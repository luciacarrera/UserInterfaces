Fairy Forms

Fairy forms or transformations are different levels of power that fairies can attain as an extention of their magical abilities. In our school, we work with our students not only to help them become full-fledged fairies, but so that they reach higher fairy forms.

Winx

This is the basic fairy form, if you have yet to unlock it, we will make sure that you do by the end of your freshmen year.

Enchantix

Enchantix is the "Final Fairy Form" in a fairy's formal education. In our curriculum, earning Enchantix is the final exam.

Higher Fairy Forms

After Enchantix, there is an infinite number of levels a fairy can reach, which are meant for specific situations. An example of this is Butterflix.
